Working Together

WorkWithMe is a site that lets you find where people are working so that you can work next to them, or lets you share what you are working on, where, and when so that others can join you.

Why?

Whether you are a developer, a designer, a student, a lawyer, an artist - it is always nice to work in good company. So because...

  • you can get help from people when you are stuck.
  • you can help others when they run into problems.
  • it's a good way to meet new people.
  • it's a good way to find people to collaborate with.
  • it's better than going to networking events.
  • it's fun.

What is coworking?

Coworking is a new trend. It started a while ago and is basically a bunch of people who cowork from the same location. Originally, people would work at cafes, in someone's office or home, or perhaps during the day at a restaurant. However, recently, there has been an amazing emergence of coworking spaces dedicated to the idea of coworking. Personally, I feel that quite often office-shares are presented as coworking, but who am I to question semantics...

If you are looking for coworking resources, you may want to check out:

  • LooseCubes.com - a site that lets you find coworking spaces.
  • WorkSnug.com - find cafes/places where you could drop anchor.
